Done. Key decisions:

- **Retryable exceptions**: `urllib.error.URLError` (DNS, connection refused, HTTP errors) and `OSError` (socket-level failures). Non-retriable things like `ValueError` on a bad URL escape immediately.
- **No sleep after the last attempt** тАФ avoids a pointless delay before raising.
- **Named `FetchError`** carries `url`, `attempts`, and the root `cause` so callers can handle or log it precisely.
- **`base_delay` param** makes the backoff testable without patching `time.sleep` constants.

Dangerous unhandled case: `resp.read().decode()` can raise `UnicodeDecodeError` (e.g. a binary response or Latin-1 page). That bubbles up immediately without retrying тАФ correct behavior, but callers may want to pass `errors='replace'` or inspect `resp.headers.get_content_charset()` first.
