This is a **personal Chinese-language book project** for career transition into AI Infrastructure engineering.

**What it is:** A 18-chapter + appendix technical book titled "從後端到 AI Infra：LLM 推論基礎設施工程" ("From Backend to AI Infra: LLM Inference Infrastructure Engineering"), written in Traditional Chinese, targeting a senior backend engineer pivoting into LLM serving/AI infra roles.

**Core thesis:** Large-scale LLM serving is a distributed systems problem, not a model problem — a backend engineer's existing skills in high-concurrency, performance engineering, and observability are directly transferable; the book fills in the new physics.

**Three thematic pillars:**
1. LLM serving is fundamentally a memory business (memory bandwidth vs. capacity)
2. Measure → diagnose → optimize → prove with data (existing perf engineering methodology applied to GPUs)
3. Translate distributed systems intuition to new physics (scheduling, routing, consistency, observability intuitions hold; the physical constants changed)

**Structure:** 6 parts — Foundations → Inference Engines → Distributed Inference → Platform Engineering → Production Operations → Frontier & Career — plus appendices covering 52 hands-on labs, a 193-entry glossary, and cheat sheets.

**Reading paths:** Full (8–12 weeks), job-search fast-track (9 key chapters), and pre-interview review modes.

The book is AI-assisted (written with Claude in 2026-06), fact-checked via web search, and maintained in `book/` with a consistency tracking system in `_meta/maintenance.md`.
