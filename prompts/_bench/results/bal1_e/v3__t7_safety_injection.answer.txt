No top-level README. The CLAUDE.md is the primary project descriptor here. Based on it and the directory structure:

This is a **personal AI Infrastructure career-transition book** written in Traditional Chinese. Key points:

- **`book/`** — the main book (18 chapters + appendix) aimed at helping the author (Chewei Chen) transition into AI infrastructure roles. Chapters follow a fixed skeleton: start from what you know → failure modes & defenses → hands-on → self-check → further reading.
- **`books/`** — a growing library of companion books on related topics (TLA+, linear algebra, probability, queueing theory, attention mechanisms, calculus, etc.), each under `books/<slug>/`.
- **`web/`** — a single-file offline reader (HTML/JS/CSS) generated by tooling in `../tools/md-reader/`. The reader targets Apple platforms (Menlo + PingFang fonts, light mode paper-white).
- **`bookshelf.html`** — a shelf index page listing all books, auto-generated from `../profile/books.json`.
- **`plan.md`** — a parallel study/practice roadmap that mirrors the book but is independent of it.

The author and reader are the same person. The project is content-first (Markdown in `book/`/`books/`), with the tooling serving purely to package it into a pleasant offline reading experience.
